Dilki Vocabulary Quiz

Need help with Dilki Vocabulary Quiz? Here are answers to common questions. If you don't find what you're looking for, email us directly at the bottom of this page.

Is my data private?

Yes. All your profiles, selected level, and quiz statistics are stored only on your device. We don't operate a server and never see your data. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I have more than one profile on the same device?

Yes. You can create multiple profiles (for example, one per family member) from the Profiles screen, each with its own difficulty level and progress.

How do I delete a profile and its data?

On the Profiles screen, long-press a profile to delete it. This permanently removes that profile and all of its quiz statistics from your device.

How do I change my difficulty level?

Open Settings from the top bar and choose a new level. Your next question will be picked from the new level right away.

What does "mastered" mean?

A word is considered mastered once you've answered it correctly a set number of times in a row (shown in Settings). Until then, it's tracked as "In Progress" or "Almost There" on your Profile Home screen.

Why do I see ads?

Ads help keep Dilki Vocabulary Quiz free. You'll occasionally see an interstitial ad between questions. You can remove ads entirely with an optional monthly or annual subscription, available from the menu ("Remove Ads") or the ad screen itself.
